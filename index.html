About Me

Hey! Name's Dimitris Boutakoglou. I'm a front-end web developer and designer from Alexandroupolis, Greece. Growing up in a cluttered balkan home made me realise the importance of simplicity and order, two values that carry over in my coding and design work. As a Gen Z kid with a slight phone/iPad addiction myself, plus a personal computer, I use multiple different devices per day. Consequently, I deeply understand how important a responsive and mobile first approach can be in order to achieve the widest possible spectrum of device availabilty, and thus I swear by it. I'm always looking for the next challenge to lose myself in, whether that'll be a new design tool, JavaScript framework or cooking recipy.
